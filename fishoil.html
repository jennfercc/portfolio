JENNIFER CREEK

UX Research & Design

Digestive Health Brand

Package & Microsite Design

Objective

A well-known digestive supplement brand planned to launch a new omega-3/fish oil product. Our team was tasked with designing the product packaging that would make the product stand out amongst competitors as a trustworthy, premium brand. We were also tasked with creating a microsite for the new product for educational purposes. In order to inform the team throughout this process, I conducted various types of research that provided insights about omega-3/fish oil supplement consumers.

*Client name has been removed for confidentiality purposes.

Consumer Research

First, I wanted to gain as much insight as I could into consumer needs within the category. Because Amazon is a reservoir of consumer feedback, I started off by examining the most common questions customers were asking about fish oil products to determine which product attributes people care about most. I also read through customer reviews across a wide range of fish oil brands to see what customers like and what they donâ€™t like. Ultimately, I used these insights to educate the team and client on which claims we should include and which ones we should emphasize.

Competitive Landscape

I also created a comprehensive list of claims made on pack across all our top competitors because I wanted to further understand the most common type of claims made in the category. This gave the team a thorough understanding of what other brands were saying so that we could best identify how we could stand apart from the crowd through packaging claims.

Market Survey

In order create a solid recommendation for the client, I wanted to validate my initial research findings through distributing a market survey. I created a questionnaire through Survey Monkey that aimed to assess how many people take fish oils, what people take fish oils for, general level of confidence in the effectiveness of fish oils, which attributes of a product people value most, and how people typically discover a fish oil brand. We used this data to solidify our recommendation regarding claims to include on pack as well as information hierarchy.

Microsite Wireframe

My next task was to create a wireframe for the fish oil product which would exist for further educating customers on the product variants, providing coupons, and capturing their email. My goal was to take all the research findings and client recommendations and integrate them into a webpage that encompassed all key aspects of the product, highlights what sets the product apart, and helps customers thoroughly understand key product indications.
